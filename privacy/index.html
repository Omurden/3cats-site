Legal

Privacy policy.

Plain-language version: we don’t collect much, because we don’t need to. Last updated July 14, 2026.

Summary

tl;dr

3Cats Studio (“we”) makes single-player, offline story games. Our games do not require an account, do not include ads, and do not collect or sell personal data. The only place we knowingly collect any personal information is if you voluntarily give it to us — by subscribing to our launch newsletter or by emailing us.

What we collect

and why
Newsletter signup

If you submit your email address through the “Get launch emails” form on this site, we store it with our email provider, Buttondown, solely to send you launch and demo announcements. We don’t send anything else, and you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email or by writing to us.

Email correspondence

If you email info@3cats.studio (press, support, or just to say hi), we keep that email and your address to reply to you. We don’t add it to any marketing list unless you separately opt in via the newsletter form.

Our games themselves

Our games run fully offline and single-player. They do not require sign-in, do not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and do not transmit gameplay data anywhere. Save data is stored locally on your device (and, where the platform offers it, in that platform’s own cloud-save service — e.g. Steam Cloud — which is governed by that platform’s own privacy policy, not ours).

Store platforms (Steam, Apple App Store, Google Play)

When you install or purchase our games through a storefront, that storefront (Valve, Apple, or Google) processes your payment and account information under its own privacy policy — we never see your payment details, and the storefront may separately collect its own platform-level analytics that are outside our control.

Children’s privacy

COPPA / GDPR-K

Our games and this site are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information (e.g. via the newsletter form), contact us at info@3cats.studio and we will delete it.

Your rights

access, deletion, questions

We’re based in the Netherlands (EU). Wherever you are, you can ask us at any time what we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or ask us to stop contacting you — email info@3cats.studio and we’ll handle it directly (there’s so little data that this is a same-day, human reply, not a form).

Changes to this policy

 

If this policy changes — for example, if a future game adds an optional online feature — we’ll update this page and the “last updated” date above.
