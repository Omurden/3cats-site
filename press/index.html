Description (long)

All Time High is a premium, text-based thriller about the hunger to get rich. You install home theaters in hillside mansions and watch other people’s money grow on the wall — until, with $200 of grocery money, you buy your first coin and feel the first hit. A boutique fund notices you. A memecoin turns a joke into a fortune. A private token offers you the inside of a room you were only ever hired to wire.

You read the story on the surfaces where the mania actually happens: a group chat, a news feed, a live trading terminal, a bank notification at 2 a.m. There’s no combat and no map — just choices, consequences, and a number that keeps climbing until it doesn’t. Four hidden variables quietly track who you’re becoming, and six distinct endings each name what it cost.

Features
  • A story told entirely through the interface — messages, feeds, email, and a market terminal you actually trade.
  • Choices with a ledger: hidden cash / greed / integrity / heat track your descent. The story remembers.
  • A scripted market you trade — buy/sell moments move your net worth and bend the story.
  • Six endings — clean exit, exile, a federal knock, the fall guy, the whistleblower, rock bottom.
  • Adaptive music that shifts with the market and the tension, layer by layer.
  • A finishable premium one-shot (2.5–3.5 h), built to replay for the endings you didn’t take.
  • Fully offline, single-player. 10 languages.
